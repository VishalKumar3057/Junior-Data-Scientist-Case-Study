Slide 6a

Model Evaluation: Threshold Tuning

The default 0.5 classification threshold is not optimal for this problem. We use a Precision-Recall Curve to find a threshold that balances business needs.

The Trade-Off

Lowering the threshold:

  • Increases Recall: We catch more potential churners. (Good)
  • Decreases Precision: We get more "false alarms". (Trade-off)

Business Rationale

The cost of missing a churner (False Negative) is much higher than the cost of a false alarm (False Positive).

Therefore, we prioritize a high Recall (~70%).

Threshold: 0.45
Selected to achieve the target recall of ~70% while maintaining acceptable precision. This ensures we proactively engage a majority of at-risk customers.
